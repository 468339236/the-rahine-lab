RHINELAB

     RHINELAB LLC. is a company located in Colombia, which is committed to expanding life science, chemical manufacturing, biological citation and other technical fields, and is also a scientific and technological group supported by Colombia. There are many undisclosed projects in Rhine Life, covering a wide range of areas. According to the media, some projects of Rhine Life have secretly expanded the recruitment scale in the past period, and there are rumors that military resources have been involved. Relevant people speculate that these confidential projects may be related to the source rock experiment targeting organisms. [1] Headquartered in the mobile city of Trimont, it has ten departments in total, led by the component department under its jurisdiction, with five scientific research departments (structure department, energy department, source rock technology application department, ecology department, scientific investigation department) and four affairs departments (business department, defense department, engineering department, and human resources investigation department).
